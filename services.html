The admission procedure begins with filling out the online application form available on the college website. After submitting basic academic details, students must upload scanned copies of their documents for verification. Once the application is reviewed, eligible students will receive a confirmation message and may be called for counseling or an interview depending on the program. The final step includes paying the admission fee to confirm the seat.

The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across multiple streams. These include B.Sc., B.Com., B.A., BBA, BCA, M.Sc., M.Com., and various diploma and certificate courses. Each program is designed to provide strong theoretical knowledge along with practical learning opportunities to prepare students for future careers.
