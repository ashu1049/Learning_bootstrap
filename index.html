After submitting basic academic details, students must upload scanned copies of their documents for verification. The final step includes paying the admission fee to confirm the seat.

Each program is designed to provide strong theoretical knowledge along with practical learning opportunities to prepare students for future careers.

cherries

Cherries are vibrant, flavorful fruits that come in sweet and sour varieties. Rich in antioxidants, vitamin C, and anti-inflammatory properties, they are widely loved not only for their taste but also for their health benefits. Sweet cherries are often eaten fresh, while sour cherries are commonly used in baking, jams, sauces, and beverages. Their juicy texture, bright color, and refreshing flavor make cherries a popular ingredient across many cuisines.
